Seeds of Solidarity



Welcome! We are so pleased to share Seeds of Solidarity Farm and Education Center programs, resources and inspiration with you.
Seeds of Solidarity “Awakens the Power to Grow Food Everywhere to transform hunger to health, and create resilient lives and communities.”

This is a place where people of all ages, genders, races, and socio-economic statuses are welcome! We are a part of a growing community of environmental and social justice farmers, organizers, and leaders.


At Seeds of Solidarity, we are aware that hundreds of years of oppression have destroyed entire communities and decades of fossil fuel burning has destroyed entire ecosystems. Seeds of Solidarity exists because we want to bring about systematic change!


Are you ready to join us, in the fight to build a better world for ourselves and for future generations of youth?



Are we currently open?


Yes! Our farmstand at 165 Chestnut Hill Road, Orange is now open. Daylight hours, self-serve, currently stocked with salad greens and more to come. Heritage Fields farm eggs and Knectel Farm maple syrup too!

We offer many free resource materials to regenerate soil, restore climate and revitalize community. Peruse archived newsletters rich with ideas and inspiration, watch our Video on the No-till Cardboard Method. Find many more free guides and articles throughout our website.

Enjoy our Recipes for Wellness Booklet with economical recipes for all seasons, healthy living tips, gardening ideas, and ample inspiration. Plus, be inspired by our book. If you want to keep in touch, follow us on social media.
